View public Telegram channels outside of Telegram

You can actually view any public Telegram channel without requiring a Telegram account. All you need to do is follow this pattern t.me/s/<channel-name> to the Telegram channel URL. This is why you can view Daily Dad Jokes and BBC Earth Daily outside of Telegram. Even my personal public channel is made available this way.
